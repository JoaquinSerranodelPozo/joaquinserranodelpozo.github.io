Joaquín Serrano del Pozo

Historian of Late Antique and Eastern Roman (“Byzantine”) culture, with a particular interest in medieval war, Christian relics, sacred objects, and the interplay between literary, material, and iconographic sources.

I completed my PhD at the Centre for Late Antique, Islamic and Byzantine Studies at the University of Edinburgh. My doctoral thesis examined the use of Christian relics in Late Roman and Byzantine war. I am currently developing a postdoctoral project on the political and military uses of Christian relics across the medieval Euro-Mediterranean world, c. 600–1200.
